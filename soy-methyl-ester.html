Product Information:

Soy Methyl Ester is a natural solvent derived from Soybean Oil. This unique organic product has been found to have a variety of uses and benefits. Soy Methyl Ester is biodegradable, virtually non-reactive, non-toxic and non-flammable. It is useful in practically every industry from industrial and agricultural to personal care products.
