ABOUT CITRUS OLEO

Service. Support. Solutions

Citrus Oleo offers high quality, eco-friendly citrus-based solvents for Household and Industrial Cleaning applications. Our world-class customer service and technical teams are always ready to support you! With warehouse locations across North, Central and South America, Citrus Oleo offers quick turnaround and delivery. Call us today, you can always expect to be greeted by a friendly voice! Our team is excited to help grow and support your business in any way we can.

Technical Experts Working for You.

At Citrus Oleo our technical team has a passion to deliver knowledge of natural, citrus-based, environmentally preferred ingredients to our customers around the world. Driven by this powerful blend of knowledge, entrepreneurship and innovation we anticipate and find creative solutions for future market challenges, trends and developments.

Providing Eco-Friendly Ingredients.

We provide access to natural, bio-based ingredients to add value to their products without harmful effects on the planet. Our aim is to deliver better ingredients for our customers, consumers and the planet that help them innovate, simplify and grow their business in a sustainable way.

Always a Friendly Voice.

At Citrus Oleo, our unique team spirit is derived from our shared passion for knowledge of the challenges that our customers face and finding solutions for them. We can achieve this optimally when every single member of our team feels respected, included, and heard; when all our colleagues can be themselves, using their entrepreneurial skills and giving their all to their work every day.
